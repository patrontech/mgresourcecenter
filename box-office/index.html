Box Office

Overview

The point of sale and box office software is built for you to quickly and securely process ticket sales that over the phone, in person, via email or through mail order. Box Office has a dedicated section in the Admin, although it is visually separate from the Admin and uses its own set of menus and navigation. This is because Box Office has been designed as a self-contained point of sale for your events, and staff who need to process transactions may not necessarily need access to your Admin.

Box Office is also available as an app for the iPad and Android tablet. (Download Box Office from the App Store and Play Store !)

Navigating Box Office

Box Office is separated into four main sections: Events, Cart, Settings and Tools.


Events

Located in the central section of Box Office, you can view a list of all of your upcoming events and select events to begin the checkout process. Based on your preference, you can set the events in this section to be displayed by the following: List, Day, Week or Month.

The filter for List, Day, Week or Month is on the top right of the events list.

Choose an event from the list to see the next available time (highlighted in green) and all times for that day in the list below. This is the default view.

Click the filter for List, Day, Week or Month on the top right of the events list to change your view. Click the filter and choose month to see a month view of that particular series.

Click the filter and choose week to see that week's view of that particular series.

After you choose your single event or event series time slot, select a time to expose the prices and inventory in that particular single event or event series time slot.

< Hover over the top portion of any price level and click to expose a tool to enter a quantity of tickets, or simply click the plus or minus sign to add or remove one ticket at a time from the cart.
