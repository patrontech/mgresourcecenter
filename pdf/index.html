Scanning Tickets

There are two ways to scan ticket barcodes or QR codes. Laser scanning, our preferred method, is better for battery life, speed, and performance.

Camera:

  1. From the main menu, tap Launch Scanner
  2. Position the ticket's QR code inside the center of the white box on the camera's viewfinder.
  3. Hold the ticket's barcode 6-8" from the scanner. If positioned correctly, the scan will automatically happen.

Laser:

  1. From the main menu, tap Launch Scanner
  2. Ready to scan will display on the screen.
  3. Hold the barcode 6-8" from the scanner. Press and hold any of the scan buttons until the laser has read the barcode
